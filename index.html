Things to do in Iowa City!

Nature:

  • Coralville Lake and Devonian Fossil Gorge
  • Lake Macbride State Park
  • Hickory Hill Park
  • Iowa Raptor Center

Museums:

  • University of Iowa Museum of Natural History
  • University of Iowa Museum of Art (3rd floor Memorial Union)
  • The Iowa Children’s Museum (in Coral Ridge Mall)

Cafés:

  • Java House (several locations, Aaron used to work there)
  • The High Ground Café (nice ethically minded coffeehouse)
  • Prairie Lights Café (the café in the upstairs of Prairie Lights, try their Houndstooth!)
  • Stumptown Coffee (haven’t been here, heard it’s good!)

Restaurants:

  • Trumpet Blossom Café (vegan)
  • The Bluebird Dinner (nice brunch options!)
  • Short’s Burger and Shine (great burgers, great beers!)
  • Masala (Indian buffet with great vegetarian options)
  • Pagliai’s Pizza (they wear cute hats)
  • Hamburg Inn No. 2 (famous diner, campaigning presidents go there, try their pie shakes!)
  • Leaf Kitchen (nice tea party and brunch options!)
  • Seoul Grill (Korean food in the Old Capital Mall)
  • Mekong (for pan-Asian cuisine)
  • El Bandito’s (Mexican food around Iowa City's ped mall)
  • India Café (Masala’s rival, also has a great lunch buffet)
  • Panchero’s (better than Chipotle because they mix the burrito fillings up!)
  • Falbo Bro’s Pizza (best after hitting the downtown bars)
  • Bruegger’s Bagels (great bagels)
  • La Regia Taqueria (great Mexican food a little further outside of Iowa City)
  • Whitey’s (awesome family owned ice cream store with lots of flavors)

Bars:

  • The Mill (has live music, pinball machines, and a good menu as well as great prices on beers)
  • George’s (known as the artist’s bar, has live jazz in the summer)
  • Foxed (known as the writer’s bar)
  • Clinton Street Social Club (nice cocktails)
  • Sanctuary (extensive beer selection, fewer students)
  • Mickey’s Irish Pub (cheap downstairs bar)
  • Deadwood (student bar)

Grocery:

  • John’s Grocery (awesome beer and wine selection with some fine cheeses and ice creams as well)
  • New Pioneer Food Co-Op (awesome co op with lots of organic and local food and good wine selection)
  • Bread Garden Market (the only grocery store in downtown IC, they have good bread and a nice lunch buffet!)
  • Hy Vee (multiple locations, 5-10 min drive away from downtown Iowa City)

Shopping:

  • Coral Ridge Mall (includes an ice skating rink, movie theatre, and giant carousel!)
  • Iowa City Ped Mall (awesome place for shopping and listening to buskers)
  • Iowa City Farmer’s Market (Saturday mornings in the parking ramp, keep an eye out for doughnuts!!)
  • Prairie Lights Books (world famous bookstore with live readings and a coffeehouse upstairs)
  • Artifacts Antiques (delightful antique store with nicknacks of all categories)
  • White Rabbit (hipster boutique extraordinaire, all merchandise is made or donated by local artists)
  • Revival (half thrift store half hip-store, check out their new soap/candle/decor shop next door)
